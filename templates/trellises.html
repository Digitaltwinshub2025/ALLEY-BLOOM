GREEN

Vertical Trellises & Medallions

Living green walls with California native plants and decorative cultural medallions — bringing nature and art together on the alley walls.

This intervention is functionally modeled within the live digital twin and evaluated using real environmental and spatial data relevant to Water Alley.

View in Digital Twin

Role in Innovation Alley

Urban Greening

Vertical trellises maximize green space in a narrow alley by growing plants upward on wall-mounted structures. This living infrastructure improves air quality, reduces heat absorption, and creates a more inviting pedestrian environment without sacrificing ground-level circulation.

Cultural Expression

Decorative medallions interspersed among the greenery add artistic and cultural elements that celebrate Pico-Union's identity. These handcrafted pieces transform functional infrastructure into meaningful community landmarks.

In the Digital Twin

Trellis structures and plant coverage are modeled in the digital twin with accurate placement along alley walls. The visualization shows how greenery softens the corridor, demonstrates irrigation zones, and illustrates how medallions create visual rhythm. Plant growth stages can be simulated to project future appearance.

Design Components

Vertical Trellises

Wall-mounted trellis systems support climbing plants and create living green walls that improve air quality, reduce heat, and add natural beauty to the corridor.

  • Modular steel trellis panels
  • Drip irrigation system
  • Native California climbing plants
  • Low maintenance design
  • Integrated planters at base

Decorative Medallions

Interspersed among the greenery, ceramic medallions add cultural and artistic elements that celebrate Pico-Union's heritage and community identity.

  • Hand-crafted ceramic pieces
  • Cultural symbols and motifs
  • Weather-resistant glazing
  • Community-designed patterns
  • Integrated LED backlighting
